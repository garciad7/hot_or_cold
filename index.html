How to Play Hot or Cold

A random number from 1 to 100 is selected by the computer. You must guess the number. Enter your guess into the number box and click submit. You can always remove your entered guess by clicking the clear button. Once you guess the correct answer, your screen will display a green circle. At that point, the game will be over. If you'd like to play another round, click your refresh button.
